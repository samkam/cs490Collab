<p>"<b>Music and Me</b>" is a 1973 single released by <a href="/wiki/Michael_Jackson" title="Michael Jackson">Michael Jackson</a> on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Motown_Records" title="Motown Records">Motown</a> label. It was the singer's second single release from the album <i><a href="/wiki/Music_%26_Me" title="Music &amp; Me">Music &amp; Me</a></i>. It reached #34 in the Netherlands and #49 in Turkey.</p>
