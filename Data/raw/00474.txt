<p>Warner Bros. has released the full press kit for Christopher Nolan’s <strong><em>Inception</em></strong> and included are 41 high resolution images and a ton of posters.  The reason the images and posters have been released is the press junket is this weekend here in Los Angeles.  What that means is…the press get to see the movie tonight.  However, while most members of the press are usually on Twitter and Facebook right after a screening, WB has sent out an email asking everyone not to write anything on any social networking site.  However, I’m sure some will break it, so expect early reviews to hit the net very soon.  Until then, hit the jump to check out the images and posters.</p>
