<p><strong>The movie starts at Dusk (about 8:30 p.m.) on Harbor Plaza at Main   Street  and Solano Street.</strong></p>
<p>Come see the movie outdoors on the beautiful Harbor Plaza in the    heart  of the Waterfront District on Main Street and Solano Street.     Bring a  blanket or low-slung chair for lawn seating, your favorite    snacks, and  enjoy the weekly show!</p>
<p>Saturday Night at the Movies are ponsored by the<a href="http://bit.ly/SCFoundation" target="_blank" title="Suisun Foundation"> Suisun City Community Services Foundation</a> through a significant grant from <a href="http://bit.ly/RUa3T0" target="_blank" title="PG&amp;E">PG&amp;E</a>, and the additional support of the <a href="http://bit.ly/1iN2wRe" target="_blank" title="Fairfield/Suisun Rotary Club">Fairfield/Suisun Rotary Club</a> and the <a href="http://bit.ly/WaterfrontBID" target="_blank" title="Suisun City BID">Suisun City Historic Waterfront BID</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Finding Nemo</strong> (PG)<span id="movie_synopsis_all" style="display: inline;"> </span></p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/finding_nemo/" target="_blank" title="Finding Nemo"><img alt="" height="260" src="http://assets.fontsinuse.com/static/use-media-items/17/16316/full-900x1300/52b02a86/pva6ds.jpeg" style="float: right; margin: 6px;" width="175"/></a></strong>In this stunning underwater adventure, with memorable characters, humor and heartfelt emotion, Finding Nemo follows the comedic and momentous journey of an overly protective clownfish named Marlin (voice by Albert Brooks) and his son Nemo (voice by Alexander Gould) -- who become separated in the Great Barrier Reef when Nemo is unexpectedly taken far from his ocean home and dumped into a fish tank in a dentist's office. Buoyed by the companionship of Dory (voice by Ellen DeGeneres), a friendly-but-forgetful Pacific regal blue tang, Marlin embarks on a dangerous trek and finds himself the unlikely hero of an epic effort to rescue his son -- who hatches a few daring plans of his own to return safely home.</p>
<p>For more about <a href="http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/finding_nemo/" target="_blank" title="Finding Nemo">Finding Nemo</a>.</p>
<p>Thank you to <a href="http://bit.ly/RUa3T0" target="_blank" title="PG&amp;E">PG&amp;E</a> and <a href="http://bit.ly/SCFoundation" target="_blank" title="Suisun Foundation">the Suisun City Foundation</a> for underwriting another summer of great outdoor movies. These events are possible only through community support of the Foundation, so use their website to donate today.</p>
