<p><i><a href="/wiki/The_Avengers_(2012_film)" title="The Avengers (2012 film)">Marvel's The Avengers</a></i> (<i>Marvel Avengers Assemble</i> in the UK and Ireland) more commonly known as <i>The Avengers</i>, is a 2012 American <a href="/wiki/Superhero_film" title="Superhero film">superhero film</a>, scripted and directed by <a href="/wiki/Joss_Whedon" title="Joss Whedon">Joss Whedon</a>, based on the <a href="/wiki/Marvel_Comics" title="Marvel Comics">Marvel Comics</a> superhero team <a href="/wiki/Avengers_(comics)" title="Avengers (comics)">of the same name</a>. The film stars an ensemble cast consisting of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Robert_Downey,_Jr." title="Robert Downey, Jr.">Robert Downey, Jr.</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chris_Evans_(actor)" title="Chris Evans (actor)">Chris Evans</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Ruffalo" title="Mark Ruffalo">Mark Ruffalo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chris_Hemsworth" title="Chris Hemsworth">Chris Hemsworth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scarlett_Johansson" title="Scarlett Johansson">Scarlett Johansson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jeremy_Renner" title="Jeremy Renner">Jeremy Renner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Hiddleston" title="Tom Hiddleston">Tom Hiddleston</a>, <a href="/wiki/Clark_Gregg" title="Clark Gregg">Clark Gregg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cobie_Smulders" title="Cobie Smulders">Cobie Smulders</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stellan_Skarsg%C3%A5rd" title="Stellan Skarsgård">Stellan Skarsgård</a> and <a href="/wiki/Samuel_L._Jackson" title="Samuel L. Jackson">Samuel L. Jackson</a>. In <i>The Avengers</i>, <a href="/wiki/Nick_Fury" title="Nick Fury">Nick Fury</a> (Jackson), director of the peacekeeping organization <a href="/wiki/S.H.I.E.L.D." title="S.H.I.E.L.D.">S.H.I.E.L.D.</a>, recruits <a href="/wiki/Iron_Man" title="Iron Man">Iron Man</a> (Downey), <a href="/wiki/Captain_America" title="Captain America">Captain America</a> (Evans), the <a href="/wiki/Hulk_(comics)" title="Hulk (comics)">Hulk</a> (Ruffalo), and <a href="/wiki/Thor_(Marvel_Comics)" title="Thor (Marvel Comics)">Thor</a> (Hemsworth) to form a team that must stop Thor's adoptive brother <a href="/wiki/Loki_(comics)" title="Loki (comics)">Loki</a> (Hiddleston) from subjugating Earth.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p><i>The Avengers</i>, produced on a budget of $220 million, was released theatrically in the United States on May 4, 2012 and grossed a worldwide total of over $1.5 billion.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BOM_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-BOM-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> The film has garnered numerous awards and nominations with most nominations recognising the film itself, the performances of the cast (particularly those of Downey, Johansson and Hemsworth) and the film's visual effects. <i>The Avengers</i> was nominated for an <a href="/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Visual_Effects" title="Academy Award for Best Visual Effects">Academy Award for Best Visual Effects</a> and a <a href="/wiki/BAFTA_Award_for_Best_Special_Visual_Effects" title="BAFTA Award for Best Special Visual Effects">BAFTA Award for Best Special Visual Effects</a>. The film was also nominated for three <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Critics%27_Choice_Movie_Awards" title="Critics' Choice Movie Awards">Critics' Choice Movie Awards</a>, five <a href="/wiki/Empire_Awards" title="Empire Awards">Empire Awards</a>, six <a href="/wiki/Nickelodeon_Kids%27_Choice_Awards" title="Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards">Kids' Choice Awards</a>, six <a href="/wiki/MTV_Movie_Awards" title="MTV Movie Awards">MTV Movie Awards</a> (winning three), thirteen <a href="/wiki/People%27s_Choice_Awards" title="People's Choice Awards">People's Choice Awards</a> (winning three), six <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saturn_Awards" title="Saturn Awards">Saturn Awards</a> (winning four), eleven <a href="/wiki/Teen_Choice_Awards" title="Teen Choice Awards">Teen Choice Awards</a> (winning two), and six <a href="/wiki/Visual_Effects_Society" title="Visual Effects Society">VES Awards</a> (winning two).</p>
