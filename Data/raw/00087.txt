<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p><i><b><a href="/wiki/The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe" title="The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe">The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</a></b></i> is a book by C.S. Lewis. It may also refer to:</p>
<ul>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/The_Chronicles_of_Narnia:_The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe" title="The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe">The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</a></i>, a 2005 fantasy film based upon the book by Lewis</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe_(1988_TV_Serial)" title="The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe (1988 TV Serial)"><i>The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</i> (1988 TV Serial)</a>, a 1988 six-episode miniseries, part of the BBC's <i>The Chronicles of Narnia</i> (TV serial)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe_(1967_TV_serial)" title="The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe (1967 TV serial)"><i>The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</i> (1967 TV serial)</a>, a ten-part serial adaptation which aired on ITV in 1967</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe_(1979_film)" title="The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe (1979 film)"><i>The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</i> (1979 film)</a>, a 1979 animated made-for-TV movie produced by Bill Melendez Productions for the Children's Television Workshop</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe_(play)" title="The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe (play)"><i>The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</i> (play)</a>, a dramatization of the novel by le Clanch√© du Rand</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Chronicles_of_Narnia:_The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe_(video_game)" title="The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe (video game)"><i>The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</i> (video game)</a>, a 2005 video game based on the 2005 film</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Chronicles_of_Narnia:_The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe_(soundtrack)" title="The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe (soundtrack)"><i>The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</i> (soundtrack)</a>, the soundtrack of the 2005 film</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Music_Inspired_by_The_Chronicles_of_Narnia:_The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe" title="Music Inspired by The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe">Music Inspired by The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</a></i>, a collection of songs by various Christian artists with the common theme of C. S. Lewis's <i>The Chronicles of Narnia</i></li>

<li>"The Lion, The Witch And The Wardrobe", an 1996 episode of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2point4_children" title="2point4 children">2point4 children</a></i>, a British sitcom</li>
<li>"<a href="/wiki/The_Doctor,_the_Widow_and_the_Wardrobe" title="The Doctor, the Widow and the Wardrobe">The Doctor, the Widow and the Wardrobe</a>", a <i><a href="/wiki/Doctor_Who" title="Doctor Who">Doctor Who</a></i> Christmas episode partly inspired by <i><a href="/wiki/The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe" title="The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe">The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</a></i>.</li>
