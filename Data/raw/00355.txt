<p><b>Kathryn Beaumont</b> (born 27 June 1938) is an <a href="/wiki/English_people" title="English people">English</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a>, <a href="/wiki/Singing" title="Singing">singer</a>, and school teacher who was born in London. She is best known for providing the voices of both <a href="/wiki/Alice_(Alice%27s_Adventures_in_Wonderland)#Disney_film" title="Alice (Alice's Adventures in Wonderland)">Alice</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Alice_in_Wonderland_(1951_film)" title="Alice in Wonderland (1951 film)">Disney's Alice in Wonderland</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Wendy_Darling" title="Wendy Darling">Wendy</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Peter_Pan_(1953_film)" title="Peter Pan (1953 film)">Disney's Peter Pan</a></i>. <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney" title="Walt Disney">Walt Disney</a> personally cast Beaumont after seeing the film <i><a href="/wiki/On_an_Island_with_You" title="On an Island with You">On an Island with You</a></i>, in which child actress Beaumont had a small role. Beaumont has also reprised her voice acting role as Alice in two episodes of the animated series, <i><a href="/wiki/Disney%27s_House_of_Mouse" title="Disney's House of Mouse">Disney's House of Mouse</a></i>, and as both Alice and Wendy in the video game <i><a href="/wiki/Kingdom_Hearts" title="Kingdom Hearts">Kingdom Hearts</a></i>.</p>
<p>In 2005, Beaumont retired as the voice of Alice and Wendy, and her role as these two characters was taken over by <a href="/wiki/Hynden_Walch" title="Hynden Walch">Hynden Walch</a>.</p>
