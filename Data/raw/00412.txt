<p><b>Joseph Francis Mazzello III</b> (born September 21, 1983) is an American actor, known for his roles as Tim Murphy in <i><a href="/wiki/Jurassic_Park_(film)" title="Jurassic Park (film)">Jurassic Park</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Eugene_Sledge" title="Eugene Sledge">Eugene Sledge</a> in the <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a> miniseries <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Pacific_(TV_miniseries)" title="The Pacific (TV miniseries)">The Pacific</a></i>, and <a href="/wiki/Dustin_Moskovitz" title="Dustin Moskovitz">Dustin Moskovitz</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Social_Network" title="The Social Network">The Social Network</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joseph_Mazzello&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mazzello was born in <a href="/wiki/Rhinebeck_(village),_New_York" title="Rhinebeck (village), New York">Rhinebeck, New York</a>, and raised in <a href="/wiki/Hyde_Park,_New_York" title="Hyde Park, New York">Hyde Park, New York</a>, the son of Virginia (née Strong) and Joseph Mazzello, Jr., who owned a <a href="/wiki/Performing_arts" title="Performing arts">performing arts</a> school.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-issuu_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-issuu-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> He has one older sister, Mary Mazzello, and a younger brother, John Mazzello, who have both appeared in films. He is of three eighths Italian, as well as Polish, Irish, and German descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-imdb_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-imdb-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> Mazzello went to Catholic school <a href="/wiki/Our_Lady_of_Lourdes_High_School" title="Our Lady of Lourdes High School">Our Lady of Lourdes</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> He is an alumnus of the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Southern_California" title="University of Southern California">University of Southern California</a>, entering the <a href="/wiki/USC_School_of_Cinematic_Arts" title="USC School of Cinematic Arts">USC School of Cinematic Arts</a> in 2001 following a recommendation letter of director <a href="/wiki/Steven_Spielberg" title="Steven Spielberg">Steven Spielberg</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup> Mazzello paid for school with his salary for a small appearance in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Lost_World:_Jurassic_Park" title="The Lost World: Jurassic Park">The Lost World: Jurassic Park</a></i> (1997), something the actor jokingly referred as "my graduation present from Spielberg".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joseph_Mazzello&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mazzello's most notable roles were as <a href="/wiki/Douglas_Gresham" title="Douglas Gresham">Douglas Gresham</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Shadowlands_(film)" title="Shadowlands (film)">Shadowlands</a></i> and Tim Murphy in <i><a href="/wiki/Jurassic_Park_(film)" title="Jurassic Park (film)">Jurassic Park</a></i>, both in 1993, Dexter in the 1995 film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Cure_(1995_film)" title="The Cure (1995 film)">The Cure</a></i>, and "Sledgehammer" Eugene Sledge in the HBO miniseries <i>The Pacific</i>. In 2010, Mazzello played <a href="/wiki/Dustin_Moskovitz" title="Dustin Moskovitz">Dustin Moskovitz</a>, one of the co-founders of <a href="/wiki/Facebook" title="Facebook">Facebook</a> in the <a href="/wiki/David_Fincher" title="David Fincher">David Fincher</a>-directed film <i>The Social Network</i>.</p>
<p>Mazzello's directorial debut was made with the film <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Matters_of_Life_and_Death" title="Matters of Life and Death">Matters of Life and Death</a></i> in which he also stars; the cast includes <a href="/wiki/David_Strathairn" title="David Strathairn">David Strathairn</a> (with whom he has worked in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Sensation_of_Sight" title="The Sensation of Sight">The Sensation of Sight</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_River_Wild" title="The River Wild">The River Wild</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Simon_Birch" title="Simon Birch">Simon Birch</a></i>), <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Gillies" title="Daniel Gillies">Daniel Gillies</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rachael_Leigh_Cook" title="Rachael Leigh Cook">Rachael Leigh Cook</a>.</p>
