<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Fictional universe of <i>Avatar</i></h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p>In the 2009 science fiction film <i><a href="/wiki/Avatar_(2009_film)" title="Avatar (2009 film)">Avatar</a></i>, director <a href="/wiki/James_Cameron" title="James Cameron">James Cameron</a> conceived a <a href="/wiki/Fictional_universe" title="Fictional universe">fictional universe</a> in which humans seek to mine <a href="/wiki/Unobtainium" title="Unobtainium">unobtanium</a> on the fictional <a href="/wiki/Exoplanet" title="Exoplanet">exoplanetary</a> <a href="/wiki/Natural_satellite" title="Natural satellite">moon</a>, Pandora. The Earth-like moon is inhabited by a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sapience" title="Sapience">sapient</a> indigenous <a href="/wiki/Humanoid" title="Humanoid">humanoid</a> species called the Na'vi, and varied fauna and flora. Resources Development Administration (RDA) scientists, administrators, recruits, support, and security personnel travel to Pandora in the 22nd century to discover this lush world, which is inhabited by many lifeforms including the human-like Na'vi. The clan with which the humans have contact in the film "[lives] in a giant tree that sits on a vast store of a mineral called <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unobtanium#Science_fiction" title="Unobtanium">unobtanium</a>, which humans want as an energy supply."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Josh_Rottenberg_2009_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Josh_Rottenberg_2009-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>The Pandoran <a href="/wiki/Biosphere" title="Biosphere">biosphere</a> teems with a <a href="/wiki/Biodiversity" title="Biodiversity">biodiversity</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bioluminescent" title="Bioluminescent">bioluminescent</a> <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> ranging from <a href="/wiki/Hexapoda" title="Hexapoda">hexapodal</a> animals to other types of exotic <a href="/wiki/Fauna" title="Fauna">fauna</a> and <a href="/wiki/Flora" title="Flora">flora</a>. The Pandoran <a href="/wiki/Ecology" title="Ecology">ecology</a> forms a vast <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Neural_network" title="Neural network">neural network</a> spanning the entire lunar surface into which the Na'vi and other creatures can connect. The strength of this collective consciousness is powerfully illustrated when the human invaders are defeated in battle by the Pandoran ecology, after the resolute Na'vi were nearly defeated. Cameron utilized a team of expert advisors in order to make the various examples of fauna and flora as scientifically feasible as possible.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LATimes_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-LATimes-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Astronomy_and_geology">Astronomy and geology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fictional_universe_of_Avatar&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Astronomy and geology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Poliphemus.png"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="300" data-file-width="300" height="220" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ad/Poliphemus.png/220px-Poliphemus.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/ad/Poliphemus.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/ad/Poliphemus.png 2x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Poliphemus.png" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Artist interpretation of Polyphemus</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>In the film, Pandora is depicted as being located in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alpha_Centauri_A" title="Alpha Centauri A">Alpha Centauri A</a> system approximately 4.37 <a href="/wiki/Light-year" title="Light-year">light-years</a> (276,000 <a href="/wiki/Astronomical_unit" title="Astronomical unit">AU</a>) away from <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">Earth</a>. It is one of the many <a href="/wiki/Natural_satellite" title="Natural satellite">natural satellites</a> orbiting the <a href="/wiki/Gas_giant" title="Gas giant">gas giant</a> Polyphemus,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> named for the <a href="/wiki/Polyphemus" title="Polyphemus">Polyphemus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Greek_mythology" title="Greek mythology">Greek mythology</a>. Pandora's atmosphere is a mix of <a href="/wiki/Nitrogen" title="Nitrogen">nitrogen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oxygen" title="Oxygen">oxygen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Carbon_dioxide" title="Carbon dioxide">carbon dioxide</a>, <a href="/wiki/Xenon" title="Xenon">xenon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ammonia" title="Ammonia">ammonia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Methane" title="Methane">methane</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hydrogen_sulfide" title="Hydrogen sulfide">hydrogen sulfide</a>. Humans cannot breathe this, so they wear Exo-Packs when outside their buildings or vehicles.</p>
<p>Leri Greer, a designer at <a href="/wiki/Weta_Workshop" title="Weta Workshop">Weta Workshop</a>, explained the unusual day/night cycle experienced by the Na'vi, who inhabit a moon orbiting a planet, that in turn orbits around a star.</p>
<blockquote class="toccolours" style="float:none; padding: 10px 15px 10px 15px; display:table;">
<p>I actually wrote about how it causes a particular sky coloration across the visible spectrum at moments of pure dusk. And the Na'vi, depending on what elevation they live at (sea level versus higher altitudes), perceive a distinct color in narrow band at the horizon at that moment. They identify themselves, and signify in their markings, with this color. Which helps other Na'vi quickly discern at a distance what/where they are from, or what they are likely to be like (fishermen, high plains, skyriders, etc.). That pure dusk "color," combined with their other predominant color markings lets you also know how they relate to Eywa as a "religion" versus Eywa as a physical reality. And during ceremonial gatherings you can "read" a Na'vi by how they mark themselves with dyes, muds, and paints. And the environment and day/night cycle is directly responsible for the development of this social expression behavior. Again, this was an internal idea to help us design things at Weta Workshop, it's hard to say how much filtered upward to the larger production.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-27questions_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-27questions-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
</blockquote>
<p>Pandora has lush, tropical <a href="/wiki/Rainforest" title="Rainforest">rainforests</a> that cover much of its continents. Pandora also possesses a lesser gravitational force than Earth does. The geology of Pandora is strongly affected by the presence of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unobtanium" title="Unobtanium">unobtanium</a>, a mineral whose <a href="/wiki/Superconductivity" title="Superconductivity">superconductive properties</a> allow it to float in magnetic fields. This property makes it highly valued by humans, who mine it for transport back to Earth. Pandora's levitating Hallelujah Mountains contain significant quantities of unobtanium, which allows them to ride the strong magnetic fields in their region.</p>
<p>Cameron hopes to explore the other moons in future sequels, books, and spin-offs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Josh_Rottenberg_2009_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Josh_Rottenberg_2009-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-carroll2006_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-carroll2006-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-carpetbagger_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-carpetbagger-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Pandoran_biosphere">Pandoran biosphere</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fictional_universe_of_Avatar&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Pandoran biosphere">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote relarticle mainarticle">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Pandoran_biosphere" title="Pandoran biosphere">Pandoran biosphere</a></div>
<p>The Pandoran biosphere teems with a biodiversity of bioluminescent species ranging from hexapodal animals to other types of exotic fauna and flora. The Pandoran ecology forms a vast neural network spanning the entire lunar surface into which the Na'vi and other creatures can connect. The strength of this collective consciousness is powerfully illustrated when the human invaders are defeated in battle by the Pandoran ecology, after the resolute Na'vi were nearly defeated. Cameron utilized a team of expert advisors in order to make the various examples of fauna and flora as scientifically feasible as possible.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Humans">Humans</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fictional_universe_of_Avatar&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Humans">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Unobtainium.PNG"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="374" data-file-width="503" height="164" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/1/1f/Unobtainium.PNG/220px-Unobtainium.PNG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/1/1f/Unobtainium.PNG/330px-Unobtainium.PNG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/1/1f/Unobtainium.PNG/440px-Unobtainium.PNG 2x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Unobtainium.PNG" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Of interest to the humans is Pandora's reserves of <a href="/wiki/Unobtainium" title="Unobtainium">unobtanium</a>, a valuable <a href="/wiki/Room-temperature_superconductor" title="Room-temperature superconductor">room-temperature superconductor</a> mineral valued at "20 million a kilo". The name is a reference to mythical materials used in engineering which have better properties than real materials.</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>In the <i>Avatar</i> universe (set in the year 2154), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Humans" title="Humans">humans</a> have achieved a technologically advanced, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Post-industrial" title="Post-industrial">post-industrial</a> society dominated by powerful corporations and industries. One of <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">Earth</a>'s most powerful corporations is the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Globally_Integrated_Enterprise" title="Globally Integrated Enterprise">globally integrated</a> Resources Development Administration (RDA), a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Quasi-governmental_organization" title="Quasi-governmental organization">quasi-governmental organization</a> that possesses a <a href="/wiki/Monopoly" title="Monopoly">monopoly</a> over all resources in the <a href="/wiki/Alpha_Centauri" title="Alpha Centauri">Alpha Centauri</a> system and any other non-Earth location. The Interplanetary Commerce Administration granted these sole rights to the RDA under the stipulation that the use of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Weapons_of_mass_destruction" title="Weapons of mass destruction">weapons of mass destruction</a> of any kind are to be strictly prohibited.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span>[</span>8<span>]</span></a></sup> Known RDA personnel on Pandora include head administrator Parker Selfridge, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Colonel_Miles_Quaritch" title="Colonel Miles Quaritch">Colonel Miles Quaritch</a>, Private Sean Fike, Corporal Lyle Wainfleet, Dr. Max Patel, Dr. Grace Augustine, Dr. Norm Spellman, and Samson 16 pilot Trudy Chacon.</p>
<p>Although Earth is never seen in the film, other than in the extended collectors edition, Cameron developed the future Earth of <i>Avatar</i> as a <a href="/wiki/Dystopia" title="Dystopia">dystopian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Human_overpopulation" title="Human overpopulation">overpopulated</a>, overpolluted, global urban slum wrecked by corrupt, nature-destroying <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Industrialism" title="Industrialism">industrialism</a>; the movie's background <a href="/wiki/Cyberpunk" title="Cyberpunk">cyberpunk</a> theme is a regular feature of his work. According to Jake, the Earth is a "dying world" where humans have "killed their <a href="/wiki/Natural_environment" title="Natural environment">mother</a>", suggesting that there is very little, if any, functioning natural ecosystem left. By the film's 22nd century timeframe, Earth faces a worldwide economic/energy crisis due to the depletion of natural resources. Earth is also apparently so politically unstable that the services of <a href="/wiki/Private_military_company" title="Private military company">private security contractors</a> and the militaries of Earth's nations are in high demand; Colonel Quaritch boasts about serving three combat tours of duty in <a href="/wiki/Nigeria" title="Nigeria">Nigeria</a> before coming to Pandora and notes that Jake is a veteran of a military operation in <a href="/wiki/Venezuela" title="Venezuela">Venezuela</a>. The planet has also suffered serious natural and man-made disasters, such as an <a href="/wiki/World_war" title="World war">intra-continental conflict</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tsunamis" title="Tsunamis">tsunamis</a> hitting the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/East_coast_of_the_united_states" title="East coast of the united states">east</a> and <a href="/wiki/West_Coast_of_the_United_States" title="West Coast of the United States">west</a> coasts of the United States.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Technology">Technology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fictional_universe_of_Avatar&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Technology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Na%27vi_Brain_1.png"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="311" data-file-width="350" height="195" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/8/87/Na%27vi_Brain_1.png/220px-Na%27vi_Brain_1.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/8/87/Na%27vi_Brain_1.png/330px-Na%27vi_Brain_1.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/8/87/Na%27vi_Brain_1.png 2x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Na%27vi_Brain_1.png" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
One of the futuristic computer screens, used for the AVATAR program, which employ 3D graphics and touchscreen interface.</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>Technologically, humans have achieved monumental advancements by 2154: <a href="/wiki/Interplanetary_spaceflight" title="Interplanetary spaceflight">interplanetary</a> and <a href="/wiki/Interstellar_travel" title="Interstellar travel">interstellar</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Interstellar_space_travel" title="Interstellar space travel">space travel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Space_colonization" title="Space colonization">colonization</a>; virtual <a href="/wiki/3D_printing" title="3D printing">3D printing</a> and <a href="/wiki/Holography" title="Holography">holography</a> mapping; and advanced methods of <a href="/wiki/Cryonics" title="Cryonics">cryonics</a> and <a href="/wiki/Psionics" title="Psionics">psionics</a> (via <a href="/wiki/Brain%E2%80%93computer_interface" title="Brain–computer interface">synthetic telepathy interface</a>) are employed. Using their capability of advanced <a href="/wiki/Genetic_engineering" title="Genetic engineering">genetic engineering</a>, humans develop "Avatar" hybrid bodies from genetically distinct modified-human <a href="/wiki/DNA" title="DNA">DNA</a> and Na'vi alien genetic material. Through <a href="/wiki/Psionics" title="Psionics">psionics</a>, genetically matched humans are then mind-linked to these "Avatars" for remote control operation. In the area of medicine, humans have developed advanced <a href="/wiki/Stem_cell" title="Stem cell">stem cell</a> <a href="/wiki/Neuroregeneration" title="Neuroregeneration">neuroregeneration</a> technology that can cure Jake's paralysis. However, in 2154, it is still extremely expensive and is not covered by <a href="/wiki/United_States_Department_of_Veterans_Affairs" title="United States Department of Veterans Affairs">Veterans Affairs</a> benefits. Thus, RDA is initially able to use Jake's desire to regain the use of his legs as leverage against him.</p>
<p>As with many science fiction films, many space vehicles, aircraft, ground vehicles, weapons and technologies were created to fit the story. Many were patterned after historical or contemporary technologies to give the film a sense of futuristic realism.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup> Concept artist Ryan Church based many drawings on aerodynamic research from previously classified NASA and DARPA technical papers. Unlike the movie <a href="/wiki/Aliens_(film)" title="Aliens (film)"><i>Aliens</i></a> which employed one drop ship from de-orbit to ground combat, several vehicles cover specific roles of utility transport, gunship, and base resupply. This is similar to the specialization of aircraft and helicopters in the United States military since the Vietnam war.</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Amplified Mobility Platform</b> is an MK-6 vehicle redesigned for Pandora's hostile environment that is worn and controlled as a large "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mech" title="Mech">mech</a>" motorized suit. They are 16-foot-tall (4.9 m) exoskeletons powered by <a href="/wiki/Ceramic" title="Ceramic">ceramic</a> <a href="/wiki/Turbine" title="Turbine">turbines</a>. Soldiers at Hell's Gate use these for patrol and for battle. AMP suits are controlled via a semi-master-slave system – with the AMP's arm motion slaved to the pilot's arm motion. Motion-sensing gloves worn by the pilot are used to control the AMP arms – the result being that the AMP's arm movements exactly match the pilot's movement. In their military role, AMP's are usually armed with 30mm GAU-90 <a href="/wiki/Autocannon" title="Autocannon">autocannons</a>, (occasionally equipped with a blade below the barrel, allowing the AMP to cut its way through denser foliage without putting the weapon aside) which the suit deploys as a soldier or Marine would hold and use a rifle. There is an ammo belt that extends into the gun, into an ammo canister on the shoulder, over the arm, into a socket at the back. The GAU-90 fires 350 rounds per minute. The AMP suit has <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/GPS" title="GPS">GPS</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thermal_imaging" title="Thermal imaging">thermal imaging</a> display, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FLIR" title="FLIR">FLIR</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Radar" title="Radar">radar</a>. Stan Winston Studios animator and Avatar Design Supervisor John Rosengrant described the AMP suit as "An Apache helicopter with legs". It also has a ceramic knife that is three feet long. Quaritch owns an AMP suit and has named it "Beyond Glory". Its GAU-90 has a dragon painted on the ammo tube.</li>
<li><b>AT-99 Scorpion Gunship</b> is the heavily armed gunship counterpart to Samson and is similar in role and appearance to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/AH-64_Apache" title="AH-64 Apache">AH-64 Apache</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mi-28" title="Mi-28">Mi-28</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UH-1_Huey" title="UH-1 Huey">UH-1 Huey</a> gunships. <a href="/wiki/Contra-rotating_propellers" title="Contra-rotating propellers">Dual co-axial</a> <a href="/wiki/Ducted_fan" title="Ducted fan">ducted-fan</a> rotor systems with a total of four prop-rotors each with three blades lead as the aircraft's primary proplusion. It drives on redundant fuel cells. Twin turbine generators driving counter-rotating annular ceramic motors. It is equipped with <a href="/wiki/Radar" title="Radar">radar</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sonar" title="Sonar">sonar</a> generators. It is armed with hex-cluster pylon-mounted .50 caliber rotary <a href="/wiki/Chain_gun" title="Chain gun">chain gun</a> systems that can rotate a full 360 degrees with 700 rounds per minute cyclic rate of fire as close range primary weapons. The Scorpion has two pair of stub-wing pylons to carry its armament. Scorpions carry 190 TK-411 WAFAR (Wrap-Around Fin Aerial Rocket) rockets in ten 19-tube rocket launchers. The use of the rocket varies according to the warhead that can range from tactical applications to impact-based sensor systems. Eight Hawkeye NPB-9 Hellfire (Helicopter Launched Fire-and-forget) missiles are used to engage air-to-air targets in conjunction with the nose-mounted sensor suite. The tail doubles as a rear stabilizer.</li>
<li><b>C-21 Dragon Assault Ship</b> is a large four-post ducted-fan <a href="/wiki/VTOL" title="VTOL">VTOL</a> heavily armed transport and gunship which can deliver AMP suits through drop doors. Quaritch led RDA forces with one of these. Its myriad arsenal of heavy machine guns, dozens upon dozens of rocket and missile pods, and door-gunners makes it the RDA's largest ship so far, with the Valkyrie shuttle being the only larger vehicle designed for extended use in the atmosphere of Pandora. Quad turbine generators drive the four-posted ceramic propellers. The Dragon is heavily armed with multiple weapons systems included grenades, rockets, missiles and point-defense Gatling cannons. Ability to deploy many troops and AMP suits, variety of medium automatic guns, ATG and ATA missiles, incendiary rockets, grenades, door gunners, plus dual and quad <a href="/wiki/Gatling_gun" title="Gatling gun">Gatling gun</a> systems. All gun and missile systems are constrained by friendly fire avoidance codes (IFF Lockout). It has twin cockpits. Its propellers counter-rotate to navigate, plus the nose-mounted radar suite. There are eight fifty-millimeter sentry guns mounted below the right cockpit.</li>
<li><b>GAV Swan</b> is a six wheeled terrain assault vehicle. Its full name is the <b>Ground Assault Vehicle JL-723</b>. It has a gyrostabilized platform so when it travels over rough terrain, the gunner platform is stabilized. The extended gunner's chair can rise up 3.6 meters above the vehicle to see over obstructions between the gun and the target, like the long neck of a <a href="/wiki/Swan" title="Swan">swan</a>, hence the name. This 6x6 is built for all terrain, and its <a href="/wiki/Permalloy" title="Permalloy">permalloy</a> armor and four turret-mounted sixty-millimeter rotary autocannons provide protection from the Pandoran environment. The wheels have little armor and are designed with a thick polymer alloy. Swans have a gyrostabilized system that rotates a full 360 degrees. Swans also have a roll bar and four rear headlights. Swans have redundant fuel cells in the rear. When the chair extends, the ammunition storage canister is located at the back.</li>
<li><b>Hell's Gate</b> is a secure <a href="/wiki/Forward_operating_base" title="Forward operating base">forward operating base</a> with airfield, resembling a "modern day <a href="/wiki/Khe_Sanh_Combat_Base" title="Khe Sanh Combat Base">Khe Sanh</a>". Slightly more than a third of the site is taken up by the shuttle runway, VTOL pads, hangars, equipment storage areas, and garage structures. The armor bay is where vehicles come to get stored, repaired or refitted. This building is the main storage area of all AMP suits. There is also a small weight room inside a weapons locker that Quaritch uses. Designed for security and rapid force deployment, the Armor Bay is the core of Hell’s Gate’s defenses. Housed inside its fortified hangar and at the ready are Dragon Assault Ships configured for forward strike operations, Scorpion Gunships, Samson Helicopters, Model 10 Slash-Cutters, an array of AMP Suits, and other attack craft including support vehicles and assorted battle-ready machinery.</li>
<li><b>ISV <i>Venture Star</i></b> or <b>Interstellar Vehicle Venture Star</b> is an interstellar <a href="/wiki/Bulk_carrier" title="Bulk carrier">bulk carrier</a> <a href="/wiki/Starship" title="Starship">starship</a> that uses <a href="/wiki/Antimatter_rocket" title="Antimatter rocket">antimatter propulsion systems</a> to enable <a href="/wiki/Interstellar_travel" title="Interstellar travel">interstellar travel</a>. It transports supplies, equipment, humans in <a href="/wiki/Suspended_animation" title="Suspended animation">cryonic hibernation</a>, refined ore, and data between Earth and Pandora, taking around six years to travel from one to the other. The <i>Venture Star</i> does not land on the planetary surface, but stations itself in orbit and rendezvous with operations on Earth and Pandora via Valkyrie shuttlecraft that dock with it. The <i>Venture Star</i> is one of twelve RDA ISV mineral carriers, continuously traveling between the Sol and Alpha Centauri A systems. According to film designer Ben Proctor:</li>
</ul>
<blockquote>
<p>The huge glowing radiators mounted to the engines dissipate their heat, and the enormously long central truss, with its own protective coolers and reflectors, protects the cargo and crew modules from the engines' heat and radiation using the simple rule of r-squared <a href="/wiki/Attenuation" title="Attenuation">attenuation</a> rather than heavy shielding. (Yes, [James Cameron] really thinks about this kind of stuff and explains it very clearly in text and in person.) The ship has a pair of centrifugal-gravity-gen modules for the crew who remain awake for the duration, which has become a pretty typical feature of quasi-realistic ship designs in movies. But one unique feature it has which directly relates to the sub-light realistic travel is a <a href="/wiki/Whipple_shield" title="Whipple shield">cascade-style shield stack</a> to protect the speeding craft from interstellar debris. Jim's [brief] completely explains this technology, apparently based on current <a href="/wiki/NASA" title="NASA">NASA</a> research, and how it obliterates potentially catastrophic particles by letting them slam through a series of thin, light shield surfaces.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-27questions_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-27questions-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
</blockquote>
<dl>
<dd>The ship was based on designs by scientist and author <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Charles_Pellegrino" title="Charles Pellegrino">Charles Pellegrino</a>, who served as science advisor on the film,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10"><span>[</span>10<span>]</span></a></sup> and deceased scientist and author <a href="/wiki/Robert_L._Forward" title="Robert L. Forward">Robert L. Forward</a>. It also resembles the spaceship <i><a href="/wiki/Discovery_One" title="Discovery One">Discovery One</a></i> from the movie <i><a href="/wiki/2001:_A_Space_Odyssey_(film)" title="2001: A Space Odyssey (film)">2001: A Space Odyssey</a></i>.</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><b>SA-2 Samson</b> is a twin-<a href="/wiki/Ducted_fan" title="Ducted fan">ducted fan</a> <a href="/wiki/VTOL" title="VTOL">VTOL</a> utility aircraft. It is armed with two door mounted machine guns and two pylons near the cockpit with <a href="/wiki/Missile" title="Missile">missile</a> pods mounted on them. The pair of ducted-fan assemblies, each with two coaxial propellers, enable the Samson of 6,000 kilograms lifting capability by manufacturers specifications, but lifting performance is rated at 150% of that on Pandora due to the thicker atmosphere. Both ducted-fan assemblies are capable of changing <a href="/wiki/Blade_pitch" title="Blade pitch">blade pitch</a> independently. From the pilot perspective, forward and aft, lift, yaw and roll authority are accomplished by similar sets of controls found in conventional helicopters. Propulsion is twin Goetz-Korman turbine generators.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11"><span>[</span>11<span>]</span></a></sup></li>
<li><b>Valkyrie TAV</b> is a delta-wing space vehicle/aircraft used to transport equipment and personnel between ISV Venture Star and Hell's Gate on Pandora and its chief purpose is to bring Unobtanium to the spacecraft. The Valkyrie's official name is <b>SSTO-TAV-37 B-class shuttlecraft</b>. The Valkyrie has a payload of up to 60 armed troops, 25 AMP suits, 25 tons of refined Unobtanium and/or supplies. Two Valkyries carried by the ISV Venture Star are used for transfer of personnel and cargo between the orbiting ISV and the surface of Pandora. It has VTOL capability by rotating its fusion engines, and like the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/C-130_Hercules" title="C-130 Hercules">C-130 Hercules</a> was adapted as a bomber to drop <a href="/wiki/Daisy_cutter_(fuse)" title="Daisy cutter (fuse)">daisy cutter</a> bombs out of the rear cargo ramp.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fictional_universe_of_Avatar&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Owen_Gleiberman" title="Owen Gleiberman">Owen Gleiberman</a> of <i>Entertainment Weekly</i> writes, "Cameron turns Pandora into a vertiginously suspended forest landscape...Jake and the sexy tribal princess Neytiri (Zoë Saldana) wow us with their fluid, prancing movements, but there's no subtext to their smoothly virtual faces."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12"><span>[</span>12<span>]</span></a></sup> Carol Kaesuk Yoon of the <i>New York Times</i> wrote that <i>Avatar</i> "has recreated what is the heart of <a href="/wiki/Biology" title="Biology">biology</a>: the naked, heart-stopping wonder of really seeing the living world".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13"><span>[</span>13<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p><i>CNN</i> reported that the film's universe has had a profound effect on the audience over their perception of Earth, and life on it, in reality. Avatar Forums posted a topic thread entitled "Ways to cope with the depression of the dream of Pandora being intangible" which received "1,000 posts from people experiencing depression and fans trying to help them cope" (a second thread was posted for more room). Philippe Baghdassarian, the site administrator, commented that, "I wasn't depressed myself. In fact the movie made me happy, but I can understand why it made people depressed. The movie was so beautiful and it showed something we don't have here on Earth. I think people saw we could be living in a completely different world and that caused them to be depressed." Many have confessed to falling to depression and harbouring suicidal thoughts, while others have expressed disgust towards humanity and "disengagement with reality." Psychiatrist Dr. Stephan Quentzel added that "Virtual life is not real life and it never will be, but this is the pinnacle of what we can build in a virtual presentation so far."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-avatar_blues_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-avatar_blues-14"><span>[</span>14<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>In February 2010, <i>CNN</i> published an article exploring the "Avatar science" (the technology linking the human mind to a remotely controlled body). Elizabeth Landau wrote, "Scientists say we are many decades, even centuries, away from making this kind of sophisticated interaction possible, if it can be done at all." A <a href="/wiki/Neuroscientist" title="Neuroscientist">neuroscientist</a> at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Pittsburgh" title="University of Pittsburgh">University of Pittsburgh</a>, Andrew Schwartz, further commented that it "shouldn't be taken as anything but fantasy."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-avatar_science_15-0"><a href="#cite_note-avatar_science-15"><span>[</span>15<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
