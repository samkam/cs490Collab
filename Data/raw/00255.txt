<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danny_Nucci&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Klagenfurt" title="Klagenfurt">Klagenfurt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a>, to a French Moroccan mother and an Italian father,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> Nucci was raised in <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> until the age of seven, when his family relocated to the United States.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>After temporarily living in <a href="/wiki/Queens" title="Queens">Queens</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York" title="New York">New York</a>, the family settled in <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>'s <a href="/wiki/San_Fernando_Valley" title="San Fernando Valley">San Fernando Valley</a>, where Nucci graduated from <a href="/wiki/Grant_High_School_(Los_Angeles)" title="Grant High School (Los Angeles)">Grant High School</a>. He married <a href="/wiki/Paula_Marshall" title="Paula Marshall">Paula Marshall</a> in 2003, with whom he has one daughter. He also has a daughter from his first marriage to Terre Bridgham. Nucci and Marshall played lovers in <i><a href="/wiki/That_Old_Feeling_(film)" title="That Old Feeling (film)">That Old Feeling</a></i> (1997).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danny_Nucci&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the 1990s, Nucci played characters who are unceremoniously killed off in three <a href="/wiki/Blockbuster_(entertainment)" title="Blockbuster (entertainment)">blockbuster</a> films — <i><a href="/wiki/Eraser_(film)" title="Eraser (film)">Eraser</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Rock_(film)" title="The Rock (film)">The Rock</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Titanic_(1997_film)" title="Titanic (1997 film)">Titanic</a></i> (as Fabrizio De Rossi, Jack Dawson's Italian friend) — which were released within 20 months of each another between 1996 and 1997. His character in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alive:_The_Miracle_of_the_Andes" title="Alive: The Miracle of the Andes">Alive: The Miracle of the Andes</a></i> (1993) survives.</p>
<p>Elsewhere in film, he starred as Spider Bomboni in <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Love_(1990_film)" title="Book of Love (1990 film)">Book of Love</a></i> (1990) and as Petty Officer Danny Rivetti in the <a href="/wiki/Gene_Hackman" title="Gene Hackman">Gene Hackman</a>-<a href="/wiki/Denzel_Washington" title="Denzel Washington">Denzel Washington</a> thriller <i><a href="/wiki/Crimson_Tide_(film)" title="Crimson Tide (film)">Crimson Tide</a></i> (1995). He played the roles of Benny Rodriguez in the straight-to-video film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Sandlot:_Heading_Home" title="The Sandlot: Heading Home">The Sandlot: Heading Home</a></i> (2007) and a <a href="/wiki/Port_Authority_of_New_York_and_New_Jersey_Police_Department" title="Port Authority of New York and New Jersey Police Department">Port Authority</a> police officer in <i><a href="/wiki/World_Trade_Center_(film)" title="World Trade Center (film)">World Trade Center</a></i> (2006).</p>
<p>Nucci appeared as Gabriel Ortega on the <a href="/wiki/CBS" title="CBS">CBS</a> <a href="/wiki/Soap_opera" title="Soap opera">soap opera</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Falcon_Crest" title="Falcon Crest">Falcon Crest</a></i> from 1988 to 1989, and as Vincent Sforza in the television miniseries <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Firestarter_2:_Rekindled" title="Firestarter 2: Rekindled">Firestarter 2: Rekindled</a></i> (2002). Other notable TV appearances include <i><a href="/wiki/Growing_Pains" title="Growing Pains">Growing Pains</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Out_of_This_World_(TV_series)" title="Out of This World (TV series)">Out of This World</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Quantum_Leap_(TV_series)" title="Quantum Leap (TV series)">Quantum Leap</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Family_Ties" title="Family Ties">Family Ties</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Zone_(1985_TV_series)" title="The Twilight Zone (1985 TV series)">The Twilight Zone</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Tour_of_Duty_(TV_series)" title="Tour of Duty (TV series)">Tour of Duty</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Snoops_(1999_TV_series)" title="Snoops (1999 TV series)">Snoops</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Just_Shoot_Me" title="Just Shoot Me">Just Shoot Me</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/House_(TV_series)" title="House (TV series)">House</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Without_a_Trace" title="Without a Trace">Without a Trace</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Criminal_Minds" title="Criminal Minds">Criminal Minds</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Mentalist" title="The Mentalist">The Mentalist</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_NY" title="CSI: NY">CSI: NY</a></i>, two episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Castle_(TV_series)" title="Castle (TV series)">Castle</a></i> and one episode of <i><a href="/wiki/Arrow_(TV_series)" title="Arrow (TV series)">Arrow</a></i>. Along with <a href="/wiki/Ernie_Hudson" title="Ernie Hudson">Ernie Hudson</a>, he co-starred in the short-lived police drama series <i><a href="/wiki/10-8:_Officers_on_Duty" title="10-8: Officers on Duty">10-8: Officers on Duty</a></i>. He provided the voice of Alberto the Chihuahua in <a href="/wiki/The_Brave_Little_Toaster_to_the_Rescue" title="The Brave Little Toaster to the Rescue">The Brave Little Toaster to the Rescue</a>.</p>
<p>In 2010, he portrayed <a href="/wiki/John_Gotti" title="John Gotti">John Gotti</a> in <i>Sinatra Club</i>, and Dante McDermott in the science-fiction film <i><a href="/wiki/Nephilim_(film)" title="Nephilim (film)">Nephilim</a></i>. In 2011, he co-starred in the mystery thriller <i><a href="/wiki/Escapee_(film)" title="Escapee (film)">Escapee</a></i>. Nucci currently plays Mike Foster on the <a href="/wiki/ABC_Family" title="ABC Family">ABC Family</a> drama <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fosters_(2013_TV_series)" title="The Fosters (2013 TV series)">The Fosters</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>Nucci portrayed Pop, the father of main character Felix Funicello, in the 2014 <a href="/wiki/Lifetime_(TV_network)" title="Lifetime (TV network)">Lifetime</a> television movie <i><a href="/wiki/Wishin%27_and_Hopin%27_(film)" title="Wishin' and Hopin' (film)">Wishin' and Hopin'</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
