<p><i><b><a class="extiw exitstitial" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Tale_of_Two_Cities" title="wikipedia:A Tale of Two Cities">A Tale of Two Cities</a></b></i> is a novel by Charles Dickens. The novel tells the story of the brutal oppression of the aristocracy over the proletariat in France, before and during the French Revolution. It follows the lives of several protagonists through these events, most notably Charles Darnay, a French once-aristocrat who falls victim to the indiscriminate wrath of the revolution despite his virtuous nature, and Sydney Carton, a dissipated English barrister who endeavours to redeem his ill-spent life out of love for Darnay's wife, Lucie Manette.
</p>

<ul><li> The <i>Two Cities</i> in the show are <a href="/wiki/The_Hydra" title="The Hydra">the Others' camp</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Beach_camp" title="Beach camp">beach camp</a>.
</li><li> Another Dickens novel, <i><a href="/wiki/Our_Mutual_Friend" title="Our Mutual Friend">Our Mutual Friend</a></i>, is also referred to in <i>Lost</i>, especially in the episode preceding "<a href="/wiki/A_Tale_of_Two_Cities" title="A Tale of Two Cities"><span title="A Tale of Two Cities (episode: 3x01)">A Tale of Two Cities</span></a>", which is "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Live_Together,_Die_Alone,_Part_1" title="Live Together, Die Alone, Part 1"><span title="Live Together, Die Alone, Part 1 (episode: 2x23)">Live Together, Die Alone, Part 1</span></a>".
</li><li> The other two Jack-centric episodes in Season 3 also take their titles from books, "<a href="/wiki/Stranger_in_a_Strange_Land" title="Stranger in a Strange Land"><span title="Stranger in a Strange Land (episode: 3x09)">Stranger in a Strange Land</span></a>" from <i><a class="new" href="/wiki/Stranger_in_a_Strange_Land_(book)?action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Stranger in a Strange Land (book) (page does not exist)">Stranger in a Strange Land</a></i> and "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Through_the_Looking_Glass,_Part_1" title="Through the Looking Glass, Part 1"><span title="Through the Looking Glass, Part 1 (episode: 3x22)">Through the Looking Glass, Part 1</span></a>" from <i><a href="/wiki/Alice%27s_Adventures_in_Wonderland" title="Alice's Adventures in Wonderland">Alice's Adventures in Wonderland</a></i>.
