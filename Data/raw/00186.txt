<h2 class="bookssg quotesIcon">Our Summary of <span class="title">The Name of the Rose</span> by Umberto Eco</h2>
<div class="topquotes synopsis">
<div class="quotes synopsis">
<div class="quote">
<p>The Name of the Rose follows a friar and his prodigy as they look for a solution to a monastery mystery. Upon arriving at a Benedictine monastery, William of Baskerville and Adso of Melk discover that a monk has apparently committed suicide. Because they are outsiders, the Abbot asks William to look into the death. Together with Adso, the two of them begin to search for clues. The setting of Umberto Eco's first novel is an Italian monastery. The year is 1327. As the story moves forward, other monks begin to die suspicious deaths. It is up to William and Adso to figure out who is behind the deaths before everyone is taken.</p>
