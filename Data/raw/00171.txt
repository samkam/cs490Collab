<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Setting">Setting</span></h2>
<p>Centred on Anne for the majority of the series, this eight-book collection is prized and loved by many. Located in a lovely spot in <a href="/wiki/Prince_Edward_Island" title="Prince Edward Island">Prince Edward Island</a>, Canada, the first two books are based in <a href="/wiki/Avonlea" title="Avonlea">Avonlea</a>, a quaint town that is based on the real-life town of Cavendish.
</p><p><i><a href="/wiki/Anne_of_the_Island" title="Anne of the Island">Anne of the Island</a></i> is set in <a href="/wiki/Kingsport" title="Kingsport">Kingsport</a> for the most part, a bustling city with a large college by the name of <a href="/wiki/Redmond_College" title="Redmond College">Redmond College</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Anne_of_Windy_Poplars" title="Anne of Windy Poplars">fourth book</a> is put in <a href="/wiki/Summerside" title="Summerside">Summerside</a>, Prince Edward Island, and <a href="/wiki/Windy_Poplars" title="Windy Poplars">Windy Poplars</a>.
</p><p><i><a href="/wiki/Anne%27s_House_of_Dreams" title="Anne's House of Dreams">Anne's House of Dreams</a></i> is located in <a href="/wiki/Four_Winds" title="Four Winds">Four Winds</a>, a beautiful spot on the shoreline of the Island. The last three books are based in <a href="/wiki/Glen_St._Mary" title="Glen St. Mary">Glen St. Mary</a>, a short distance from Four Winds.
</p>
