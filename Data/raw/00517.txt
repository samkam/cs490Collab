					Elvis Presley live in concert at The O2 Dublin &amp; Odyssey Arena Belfast confirmed for March 2012!					</a>
</h1>
<div class="entry-head">
<span class="date ie6fix">Sep 07, 2011</span>
<span class="comments ie6fix"><a href="http://musicscene.ie/2011/09/elvis-presley-live-in-concert-at-the-o2-dublin-odyssey-arena-belfast-confirmed-for-march-2012/#comments" title="Comment on Elvis Presley live in concert at The O2 Dublin &amp; Odyssey Arena Belfast confirmed for March 2012!">3 Comments</a></span>
<span class="author ie6fix">by <a href="http://musicscene.ie/author/bren_music/" title="Posts by Musicscene">Musicscene</a></span>
</div>
<div class="entry-text">
<div class="entry-previewimage rounded preloading_background"><a href="http://musicscene.ie/musicScene/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/Elvis_presley_live_in_Concert_at_TheO2_Dublin__Odyssey_Arena_Belfast_confirmed_for_March_2012_information_details_about_shows_gigs_concerts_buy_tickets_available_irish_tour_dates_music_scene_ireland.jpg" rel="lightbox[grouped]" title="Elvis Presley live in concert at The O2 Dublin &amp; Odyssey Arena Belfast confirmed for March 2012!"><img alt="" height="180 " src="http://musicscene.ie/musicScene/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/Elvis_presley_live_in_Concert_at_TheO2_Dublin__Odyssey_Arena_Belfast_confirmed_for_March_2012_information_details_about_shows_gigs_concerts_buy_tickets_available_irish_tour_dates_music_scene_ireland-180x180.jpg" title="Elvis Presley live in concert at The O2 Dublin &amp; Odyssey Arena Belfast confirmed for March 2012!" width="180"/></a></div>
<p>‘Elvis Presley In Concert’ is an astounding production that reunites former Elvis band mates live on stage with a state of the art video projected Elvis figure. In 2012, the concert returns for a tour through Europe including live concert dates in Belfast and Dublin. Elvis Presley in Concert will be performed live at <a href="http://musicscene.ie/2010/10/the-odyssey-arena/" target="_blank">The Odyssey Arena in Belfast</a> on March 13<sup>th</sup> 2012 followed by <a href="http://musicscene.ie/2010/09/the-o2-3/" target="_blank">The O2 in Dublin</a> on March 14<sup>th</sup> 2012. Tickets for these live shows in Ireland will be on sale through <a href="http://www.ticketmaster.ie/" target="_blank">Ticketmaster</a> and usual outlets nationwide from Thursday the 8<sup>th</sup> of September 2011. <a href="http://www.ticketmaster.ie/search?tm_link=tm_header_search&amp;q=elvis&amp;search.x=23&amp;search.y=18" target="_blank">Click here to buy tickets.</a></p>
<p>In his lifetime, Elvis’ only concerts outside the United States were five shows in three Canadian cities in 1957. A world tour was an unrequited dream for Elvis and his fans.</p>
<p>The show’s concept is to present an authentic as possible Elvis Presley concert to fans around the world. The producers edited together a collection of Elvis’ finest concert performances that exist on film and video and removed virtually all sound from the footage except for Elvis’ vocal. The Elvis footage is projected on a large video screen. On stage a 16-piece orchestra and some of Elvis’ original bandmates from the concert era of his career and other cast members perform live with the Elvis video. From the first song it’s magic. You’re at a real Elvis concert.</p>
<p>Elvis Presley in Concert is a production of SEG Events, in association with Elvis Presley Enterprises, Inc.</p>
