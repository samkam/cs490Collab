<p>While the release of <strong>Christopher Nolan</strong>’s <strong><em><a href="http://collider.com/tag/the-dark-knight-rises" target="_blank">The Dark Knight Rises</a></em></strong> nearly a week away, Warner Bros. looks to be beginning an onslaught of clips from the film much to the chagrin of those of you trying to stay in the dark.  <a href="http://collider.com/dark-knight-rises-clip/179068/" target="_blank">The first clip</a> was released yesterday, and today two more clips have landed online.  One clip features <strong>Anne Hathaway</strong>’s Selina Kyle conversing with Bruce Wayne, while the other clip has <strong>Morgan Freeman</strong> showing off some new Batman equipment.</p>
<p>If you’re interested in seeing even more from <em>The Dark Knight Rises</em>, hit the jump to check out the clips.  As you well know, the film opens on July 20th.<span id="more-179580"></span></p>
<p>Here are the two new clips, followed by the first clip that was released yesterday.</p>

<p style="text-align: left;">Warner Bros. Pictures’ and Legendary Pictures’ “The Dark Knight Rises” is the epic conclusion to filmmaker Christopher Nolan’s Batman trilogy.</p>

<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>But everything will change with the arrival of a cunning cat burglar with a mysterious agenda. Uslan, Kevin De La Noy and Thomas Tull, with Jordan Goldberg serving as co-producer. The film is based upon characters appearing in comic books published by DC Comics. Batman was created by Bob Kane.</p>
