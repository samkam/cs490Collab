<p><b>46610 Bésixdouze (1993 TQ1)</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Asteroid" title="Asteroid">asteroid</a> belonging to the <a href="/wiki/Asteroid_belt" title="Asteroid belt">asteroid belt</a> between <a href="/wiki/Mars" title="Mars">Mars</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jupiter" title="Jupiter">Jupiter</a>.</p>
<p>It was formally pinpointed by <a href="/wiki/Kin_Endate" title="Kin Endate">Kin Endate</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kazuro_Watanabe" title="Kazuro Watanabe">Kazuro Watanabe</a> on October 15, 1993.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lurtz_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-lurtz-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>The name was suggested by F. Hemery and <a href="/wiki/Ji%C5%99%C3%AD_Grygar" title="Jiří Grygar">Jiří Grygar</a> as a reference to the French novella <i><a href="/wiki/The_Little_Prince" title="The Little Prince">The Little Prince</a></i>. The title character lived on an asteroid named B612, which is the number 46610 written in <a href="/wiki/Hexadecimal" title="Hexadecimal">hexadecimal</a> notation. Bésixdouze is one way to pronounce B612 in French (lit. 'B six twelve').</p>
