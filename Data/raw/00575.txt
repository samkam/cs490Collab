<div class="articleBody" itemprop="articleBody"><div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_399218" style="width: 1010px"><a href="http://entertainthis.usatoday.com/2015/02/22/with-kids-like-these-crushing-kashmir-who-needs-led-zeppelin/xxx-led-zeppelin-e-mus-cel-gbr/#main" rel="attachment wp-att-399218"><img alt="Robert Plant, John Paul Jones and Jimmy Page in 2007. (Kevin Westenberg/Getty Images)" class="size-full wp-image-399218" height="667" src="https://usatlife.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/led_zeppelin_live_16_52047561.jpg?w=1000&amp;h=667" width="1000"/></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Robert Plant, John Paul Jones and Jimmy Page in 2007. <p>The members of Led Zeppelin may not have any interest in playing together these days, but the 7- to 12-year-olds of the Louisville Leopard Percussionists are ready to take on the Zeppelin catalog. The percussion ensemble, consisting of about 55 kids from the Louisville, Ky., area, recently posted an impressive video of the group rehearsing three Zeppelin classics: <em>Kashmir</em>, <em>Ocean</em> and <em>Immigrant Song</em>.</p>
<p><span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;"><iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="593" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/JYuOZnAqQCY?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="1000"></iframe></span></p>
<p>Zeppelin guitarist Jimmy Page even posted the video to his Facebook page over the weekend, saying it was “too good not to share.”</p>
<p><fb:post href="https://www.facebook.com/jimmypage/posts/10153137369247612"></fb:post></p>
<p>Last year, the group posted a <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rHRPLZ2ZGPY">steel-drum medley</a> of We the Kings’ <em>Check Yes Juliet</em> and Pharrell Williams’ <em>Happy</em>.</p>
