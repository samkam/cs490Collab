<td>This is the so-called british Lion King Studio Album. It is also available
in German as hardcover version (Very nice!) and as cheap version with lower
quality translation, and there is a lower quality 2-parter version by Marvel
comics in the US (released in July 1996). Each file has a size of 100-200k.
All together 46 pages. There are differences between European and the US
version, a few frames are different.
<br><b><font size="-1">UK version (by Ravette Publishing): ISBN 1-85386-333-5
; 3.50 UKP (12 DM)</font></b></br></td>
